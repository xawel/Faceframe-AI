Support



Need help? We're here for you! Explore our FAQs below or reach out to our support team for assistance.





Can Faceframe track employee attendance in real-time, and does it provide reporting or analytics features for management?

Yes, Faceframe tracks employee attendance in real-time and provides comprehensive reporting and analytics features for management. Administrators can access attendance records, view trends, and generate reports to analyze employee attendance patterns.



How does Faceframe handle situations where there are multiple employees in the frame during facial recognition?

Faceframe is equipped to handle scenarios where multiple employees are present in the frame during facial recognition. The system distinguishes between individuals and accurately identifies each employee based on their unique facial features.



Is Faceframe compatible with different types of cameras or devices for facial recognition, or does it require specific hardware?

Faceframe is designed to be compatible with various types of cameras and devices for facial recognition. While specific hardware requirements may vary, the software can be configured to work with a wide range of camera setups, including built-in webcams, IP cameras, and dedicated facial recognition devices.



Can Faceframe detect and prevent spoofing attempts, such as using photos or videos to trick the facial recognition system?

Faceframe incorporates advanced anti-spoofing techniques to detect and prevent spoofing attempts. These techniques include liveness detection algorithms that analyze facial movements and behavior to ensure that the person being scanned is live and not a static image or video.



Does Faceframe support facial recognition for users wearing masks or other facial coverings?

Faceframe can adapt to recognize individuals wearing masks or other facial coverings. The system leverages advanced facial recognition algorithms that are trained to detect and analyze facial features even when parts of the face are obscured.



How does Faceframe handle scenarios where employees may have disabilities or facial differences that affect facial recognition accuracy?

Faceframe is designed to be inclusive and accommodating to individuals with disabilities or facial differences that may affect facial recognition accuracy. The system can be customized to adjust recognition thresholds or accommodate alternative authentication methods to ensure accessibility for all users.
